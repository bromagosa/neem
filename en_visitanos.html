Visit us

In order to be able to see from close by the work that A. Neem Bangalore carries out in India with the actual leprosy community and their families, we invite you to come and see for yourself, the progress we make daily, thanks to the people that support us in this dignifying work with the leprosy sick people. For this purpose, we would like to make available to you, all the necessary information that can be of great interest when you decide to visit us.

Who can visit us?

All those interested in our work, our godfathers, godmothers and associates.

The Visit

Our projects can be visited in a period of three days, during which you will have the opportunity to visit St. Anthony School in Shanti Nilayan, our childcare center in Indirapuram and the leprosy community in Chikkanayakanahalli. In case you have some time left, we can also provide your visit to the hospital of St John´s Medical Collegue, one of the largest in Bangalore, The Religious Community of Holy Teresa of Jesus, etc. Visits will be carried out preferably on weekdays. We would also like to mention to our visitors that, for being such a small association, you will get the chance to be in direct contact with the people and culture of this country and be able to enjoy their amiability and houses. So we kindly request you to cautiously choose how to dress and avoid offending the Indian culture. (Long pants in the case of men and undershirt in the case of women). If you are interested in coming, we kindly request you to contact us in advance and specify how many, when and for how long you intend to come. In that case, you can send us an e-mail to the following: info@neembangalore.org

Spend some time with us

To be able to collaborate with us, we seek people with initiative, compromised, with a minimum of 3, 6 or 12 months availability to stay, with good English notions and know-how in areas such as: health, economy, teaching, accounting and if possible, experience in development cooperation. In case of interest, please send us your curriculum and motivation letter to: info@neembangalore.org

Requirements for entrance in India

  • Documentation: Passport valid for the minimum 6 months.
  • Visa: to enter the country you will need a specific visa according to the activity that you intend to do. You will be able to find out all about visas at:
    • WEB OF THE EMBASSY OF THE INDIA www.embassyindia.es
    • WEB OF INDIA VISAS www.indiavisados.com
  • Vaccine: there is not any obligatory vaccine to enter the country, but yet it is recommended to be vaccinated against: Tetanus, Typhus, Hepatitis TO and B or Rage. For more information, visit your family doctor or check at:
    • VACCINATION www.vacunas.org
  • Currency: 1 EUR = 63,00 INR (rupees) approx. At the airport the exchange rates are always a little low. So, we recommend you not to change a lot. In Bangalore there are ATMS banks and exchange counters everywhere.
  • Some orientating prices: 1 lt water bottle – 15/20 INR; 1 chai 6/10 INR; taxi from the Bangalore airport to Indiranagar 650 INR (pre-paid taxi); double room with private bathroom in the Indiranangar area, as of 1.800 INR
